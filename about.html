What We Do

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect students to sales careers. Given that 75% of business school graduates find themselves in customer-facing roles post-graduation, the Sales Club is providing key resources to keep Bulldogs ahead of the competition. We are not just a club, we are the bridge from college to career for students, run by students.

We host workshops, guest speakers, and collaborative projects every week.
